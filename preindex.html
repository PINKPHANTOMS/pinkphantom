PINK PHANTOM

What is Pink Phantom? That is the question; that self-legitimating question of existence. What is...?

Pink Phantom is not a band, not an artist, not even human...an idea? Possibly. We are a symbol for Pink Phantom, a stand-in for the extra-linguistic, extra-phenomenal thing we might call Pink Phantom.

The project of Pink Phantom is simple: to express thought provoking and engaging ideas through art, with the hope that we can convincingly urge you to be a critical thinker; to passionately explore and evaluate the world and yourself in all of its unfathomable, infinite complexity.

Our mission is to spread power in what we believe to be its purest form: a hunger for knowledge and a will to question.

Are you Pink Phantom?

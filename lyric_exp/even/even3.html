PINK PHANTOM DOT I.O.

This is a direct quotation from Inception(2010). It is a repeated throughout the film and is an important question that is explored in the film... What is.. real? The train metaphorizes death and death is waking from the dream but you can't know for sure if this will be waking or dying... but what is the difference? The end of one world is implied in both scenarios and the steps taken into the next world are those of the utmost uncertainty.
